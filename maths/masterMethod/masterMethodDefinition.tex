$\textrm{If you have a recurrence relation of the following form: }$ \\ \\
\begin{gather*}
T(n) = a T(\frac{n}{b}) + O(n^d) \\ \\
\end{gather*}
a $>$ 0, b $>$ 1, d $\geq$ 0 \\ \\
$\textrm{Then: }$ \\ \\
\begin{gather*}
  T(n)=\begin{cases}
    O(n^d), & d > \log_b(a) \\
    O(n^d\log(n)), & d = \log_b(a) \\
    O(n^{\log_b(a)}), & d < \log_b(a)
  \end{cases}
\end{gather*}
